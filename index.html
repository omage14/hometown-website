Visit San Luis Potosí

San Luis Potosí is a city in central Mexico.

Top three activities to do at San Luis Potosí

Visit Xilitla and sótano of the Huahuas

Enjoy a Mexican lunch at a local restaurant and enjoy some relaxed free time discovering the city of Xilitla independently.

Visit Puente de Dios and Tamasopo

Swimming hole known for its turquoise water, fed by a small waterfall & surrounded by rainforest.

Cathedral of San Luis Potosi

It is located in the historic center of the state capital, on the eastern side of the main square
